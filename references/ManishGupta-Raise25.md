# 🎥 RAISE’25: The Transformative Power of AI & Open Challenges

**Speaker:** Dr. Manish Gupta, Google DeepMind
**Context:** Keynote at L&T Finance RAISE’25 event.

## 🌟 Executive Summary
Dr. Manish Gupta explores the shift from AI being a tool for productivity to becoming a driver of scientific discovery and human-centric solutions. He highlights major breakthroughs like AlphaFold and GraphCast, the evolution of foundation models (Gemini), and the specific challenges and opportunities for AI in the Indian context, particularly in language inclusion and agriculture.

---

## 🧬 AI for Science
AI is increasingly being awarded Nobel Prizes (Chemistry and Physics) for its role in fundamental breakthroughs.
- **[[AI-for-Science|AlphaFold]]**: Predicting protein structures with experimental ground-truth accuracy.
- **GraphCast**: AI-based weather forecasting that outperforms conventional numerical models in accuracy and speed.
- **Matrioska Transformers**: Techniques to make models more efficient by using lower-dimensional representations without sacrificing performance.

---

## 🤖 Evolution of Models & Agents
- **Foundation Models**: Transition from BERT to PaLM and [[Gemini]].
- **Multimodal Capabilities**: Native multimodality in Gemini allows for seamless interaction across video, audio, and text.
- **Agents**: Project Astra and Project Mariner represent the move towards autonomous agents that can see, hear, and interact with the world in real-time.

---

## 🇮🇳 AI in the Indian Context
### [[InclusiveAI|Inclusive AI & Indic Languages]]
- **The Challenge**: Only 10% of Indians are proficient in English. Most AI models are English-centric.
- **Project Vani**: A collaboration to map the diverse dialects and languages of India.
- **Project Vaani**: Open-sourcing 14,000+ hours of speech data across 80 districts.
- **Karya**: An initiative providing fair-wage data annotation work to rural populations.

### Sectoral Impact
- **Agriculture**: Using AI for pest detection and yield optimization.
- **Education**: AI-powered personalized tutoring in local languages.
- **Healthcare**: AI assisting in screening (e.g., diabetic retinopathy).
