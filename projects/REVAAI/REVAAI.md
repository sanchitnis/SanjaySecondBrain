# 🧠 REVAAI

The REVAAI project is an initiative at REVA University focused on the strategic adoption and impact analysis of Artificial Intelligence across education, science, and society.

## 🎯 Key Objectives

- **Strategic AI Ingestion**: Collect and distill cutting-edge AI research and expert talks.
- **Educational Transformation**: Explore AI-powered personalized tutoring and localized language support.
- **Social Impact**: Analyze how AI can be made [[InclusiveAI|inclusive]] for India's linguistic diversity.

## 📈 Insights & Intelligence

### 🌟 Key Ingestions
- **[[ScientificAI|Scientific AI]]**: Deep dive into **AlphaFold** and **GraphCast** as fundamental scientific pillars.
- **[[IndicLinguisticInclusion|Indic Linguistic Inclusion]]**: Detailed strategy for **Project Vaani** and ethical data work via **Karya**.
- **[[ElasticAI|Elastic AI]]**: Technical notes on **Matryoshka Transformers (Matformers)** for efficient computation.
- **[[AgenticFuture|The Agentic Future]]**: Transition from LLMs to autonomous **Agents** (Astra, Mariner).
- **[[SocialImpactSolutions|Social Impact Solutions]]**: AI applications in Indian Agriculture, Healthcare, and Education.

### RAISING AI (2025)
Insights from [[references/ManishGupta-Raise25|Dr. Manish Gupta's RAISE'25 talk]]:
- **Agentic Shift**: Transition from interactive LLMs to autonomous agents (Astra, Mariner).
- **Indic AI**: The critical need for mapping 1.4B people across dialects via [[Project Vani]].

## 🚦 Status

- **Phase**: Ingestion & Synthesis
- **Priority**: High (REVA AI Hub development)
