# 🌱 Social Impact Solutions: AI for National Challenges

Dr. Manish Gupta's RAISE'25 keynote emphasized that India's "once-in-a-lifetime" opportunity is to use AI to solve domestic socio-economic hurdles. This is achieved through targeted sectoral applications.

## 🌾 Agriculture: Pest Detection
AI is transforming subsistence farming into data-driven agriculture.
- **Problem**: Pests can destroy entire crops before a farmer even realizes the infestation.
- **Solution**: Collaborations (such as with **Wadhwani AI**) use computer vision on basic smartphones.
- **Impact**: 
    - Farmers photograph traps; AI identifies the pest count and species (e.g., Pink Bollworm in cotton).
    - AI provides immediate advice on whether to spray pesticides, saving money and reducing chemical usage.
    - Result: Increased yield and reduced input costs for smallholder farmers.

## 🏥 Healthcare: Diagnostic Access
Scaling specialized healthcare to 1.4 billion people requires AI-assisted screening.
- **Diabetic Retinopathy**: AI models can analyze retinal scans to identify early signs of blindness. 
- **Scale**: These automated tools can screen thousands of patients in districts where specialized ophthalmologists are unavailable.
- **ARDA**: Automated Retinal Disease Assessment (ARDA) by Google Research is a prime example of this technology in action.

## 📚 Education: Personalized Vernacular Tutoring
AI is bridging the gap in quality education across linguistic divides.
- **Vernacular Tutors**: LLMs adapted for Indian languages act as 24/7 personal tutors.
- **Context**: Explaining complex STEM concepts in native dialects so that learning is not hindered by the English language barrier.
- **Implementation**: Partnerships with local educational platforms to integrate AI-driven personalized feedback loops.
