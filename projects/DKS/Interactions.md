- **The Guru's Role**: Faculty must stop being "monarchs" and transmitters of dated information. AI will soon provide better information than any teacher can; hence teachers must move toward a mentorship (Guru) role focused on intuition and ethics.

**Mr. Rajan (Post 1018-1020 - 18/03/26 to 20/03/26)**

- **DKS Perspective**: Swimming with the tide is for ordinary people. To disrupt with a startup, you must have *Viveka* (discrimination). I encounter more problems with people's egos than with technology. Success involves avoiding "monopolistic syndromes" in software and trusting distributed user-led innovation over central control.
