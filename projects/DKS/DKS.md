# Prof. D.K. Subramanian (DKS)

Prof. D.K. Subramanian is a distinguished academic and researcher, formerly a Dean and faculty member at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc). His work spans computer science research, government consultancy, and philosophical synthesis.

## Knowledge Repository

This project contains the distilled wisdom and memoirs of DKS, organized into thematic areas:

- [[projects/DKS/Memoirs|📜 Memoirs & Career Highlights]] — Chronological history of his life, IISc career, and government work.
- [[projects/DKS/Philosophy|🧠 Philosophical Reflections]] — Thoughts on Stoicism, the Gita, Indian vs. Western systems, and conscious living.
- [[projects/DKS/Technology_and_Society|🌐 Technology & Society]] — Insights on AI, Infrastructure, Telecom, and the future of Education.
- [[projects/DKS/Interactions|💬 Dialogues & Interactions]] — Recorded exchanges with Sanjay Chitnis and other peers.

## Key Themes

- **The Importance of Reading**: DKS emphasizes reading as a foundational habit for leaders and thinkers.
- **Stoicism vs. Sthitaprajna**: Comparative analysis of mental detachment and detachment from outcomes.
- **Technology Adoption**: Critical views on how India adapts (or fails to adapt) to global technological shifts.
